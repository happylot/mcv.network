| Breakpoint | Layout |

| /publishers/monetization | "website monetization", "publisher ad revenue" | 🟢 Phase 4 |

### Technical SEO

- **SSR/SSG** (Next.js) cho tất cả marketing pages
- **Core Web Vitals**: LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1
- **Schema markup**: Organization, Product, FAQ, BreadcrumbList
- **Sitemap.xml**: Auto-generated, submitted to Google/Bing
- **Multi-language**: `/en/`, `/vi/`, `/es/` (Phase 3+)
